HOW TO PICK A SECTORAL ETF
Understand the economic cycle position

Sectoral ETFs are inherently cyclical â€” their returns depend heavily on the macroeconomic phase. Banking ETFs perform best during rate-cut cycles when NIMs expand. Metals ETFs do well during global commodity super-cycles. IT ETFs are defensive in domestic slowdowns (USD revenues) but suffer during global tech slowdowns. Defence ETFs benefit from sustained government capex regardless of economic cycle. Map the sector to the current and expected macro environment before investing.

Check expense ratio and liquidity

Sectoral ETFs have higher expense ratios (0.15-0.60%) than broad-market ETFs (0.04-0.10%). Since you're expressing a tactical view, limit sectoral ETF allocation to 5-15% of your portfolio. Check the average daily volume â€” thin volumes create wide bid-ask spreads that erode returns.
