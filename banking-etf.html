NIFTY BANK ETF vs PRIVATE BANK ETF vs PSU BANK ETF
BANKBEES — Broadest banking exposure

Nifty Bank Index includes the 12 most liquid Indian bank stocks across both private and public sector. BANKBEES (0.20% expense) is the go-to product for investors who want banking sector exposure without choosing between private and PSU banks. Currently dominated by HDFC Bank (~30%), ICICI Bank (~21%), Axis Bank (~11%) and Kotak Bank (~10%) — effectively ~80% private banks even in the composite index.

PVTBNKETF — Pure private sector banking

Tracks the Nifty Private Bank Index — concentrating on HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank and IndusInd Bank. Private banks have consistently delivered superior ROE (15-18%) vs PSU banks (12-14%) due to better management, lower NPAs and stronger tech platforms. Best for investors bullish on India's private banking sector specifically.

PSUBNKBEES — Value play on government banks

PSU Bank ETFs are India's quintessential cyclical value play. These banks trade at 0.8-1.2x book (vs private banks at 2.5-4x) with dividend yields of 3-5%. After the massive NPA cleanup cycle (2015-2020), PSU banks have restored profitability — SBI delivered 15%+ ROE in FY24. Best held as a 5-10% tactical allocation during monetary easing cycles when PSU bank NPLs typically fall.
