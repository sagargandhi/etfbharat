Privacy Policy

How ETFBharat collects, uses and protects information when you visit our site.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

OVERVIEW

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

ETFBharat.com ("we", "our", or "us") is a free educational platform about Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) listed on NSE and BSE in India. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

By using ETFBharat.com you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our site, certain information is collected automatically by third-party analytics tools:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Browser type, operating system and device type
  • Referring URL (how you arrived at our site)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level — not precise)
  • Screen resolution and language settings

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information through our website forms or registration, because ETFBharat has no account system — the site is fully public and free.

COOKIES

How We Use Cookies

ETFBharat.com uses cookies — small text files stored in your browser — for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve content and navigation.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): used to show relevant ads. Google may use these cookies to personalise ads based on your browsing history.
  • Functional cookies: remember your preferences such as the dismissed disclaimer bar.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some site features. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

GOOGLE ANALYTICS

Analytics & Measurement

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to measure traffic and engagement. Google Analytics collects data such as session duration, pages per visit, and traffic sources. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual users. Data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

GOOGLE ADSENSE

Advertising

ETFBharat.com may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our site and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

THIRD-PARTY LINKS

External Websites

Our site may contain links to AMC websites, NSE/BSE, SEBI, and other external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.

DATA RETENTION

How Long We Keep Data

We do not store any personal data on our own servers. Analytics data retained by Google Analytics follows Google's default retention settings (14 months for user-level data). Advertising data is managed by Google and subject to their data retention policies.

YOUR RIGHTS

Control Over Your Data

Since ETFBharat does not collect personally identifiable information, most data rights (access, correction, deletion) are handled through Google's own tools. You can:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics via browser add-on
  • Opt out of personalised ads via Google Ads Settings
  • Clear site cookies via your browser settings
  • Contact us at the address below for any other privacy concerns
CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of ETFBharat.com after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

CONTACT

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
